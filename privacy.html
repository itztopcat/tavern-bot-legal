Privacy Policy

Adventure Frontier Tavern Bot. Last updated: June 10, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the Adventure Frontier Tavern Bot (“the Bot”, “we”, “us”) collects, uses, and stores information when you use our Discord features, including Community Tower, the crate request system, Westward Bound, and other Tavern minigames.

1. Who operates this bot

The Bot is operated by the Adventure Frontier / The Tavern community project. For privacy questions or data requests, contact the bot owner through the Adventure Frontier Discord server.

2. Information we collect

When you interact with the Bot, we may store:

  • Discord identifiers: your user ID, username/display name, and guild (server) ID where the Bot is used.
  • Community Tower: in-game progress such as floors built, coins, XP, level, inventory, boss participation, pets, professions, prestige/season stats, and leaderboard-related activity.
  • Crate system: crate/chest request timestamps, request message IDs, approval records, and (for Tavern Guards) moderation stats such as approval counts, response times, streaks, achievements, and optional wellness break status.
  • Other minigames (e.g. Westward Bound, Telekinesis): game state, scores, and progress needed to run those features.
  • Vote rewards: if you vote for our server on top.gg, we may record that you claimed a vote reward to prevent abuse.

We do not collect your email, password, payment information, or the content of your private messages outside what Discord provides to the Bot in channels where it is active.

3. How we use information

  • To provide game features, leaderboards, achievements, and server-specific tools.
  • To prevent abuse (duplicate approvals, vote fraud, etc.).
  • To send optional Bot DMs (e.g. crate delivery notices, guard wellness check-ins) where the feature requires it.
  • To improve and maintain the Bot.

4. Where data is stored

Data is stored in a MongoDB database on servers controlled by the Bot operator. Data is kept only as long as needed for the features above or until you request deletion (see Section 6).

5. Sharing with third parties

We do not sell your personal data. We use:

  • Discord: to run the Bot (subject to Discord’s Privacy Policy).
  • MongoDB / hosting providers: to store game and moderation data.
  • top.gg: only if you use vote rewards; governed by their policies when you vote.

6. Your rights & data deletion

You may request access to or deletion of your stored Bot data by contacting the bot owner in the Adventure Frontier Discord. We will respond within a reasonable time. Note that deleting data may reset your in-game progress.

If you leave a server, your data may remain until manually removed or reset by a season/prestige mechanic.

7. Children

The Bot is intended for Discord users who meet Discord’s minimum age requirements. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date at the top will change when we do. Continued use of the Bot after changes means you accept the updated policy.

9. Contact

Questions: open a ticket or message staff in the Adventure Frontier Discord.
